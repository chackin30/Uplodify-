Everything You Need to Know About CheckIn30

1. Simple Registration Process
Getting started is completely free and takes less than 2 minutes. Just provide your name, email address, and UPI ID. Your UPI ID is where all your rewards will be sent directly. We never share your personal information with anyone. After registration, you'll receive a confirmation on your email and a welcome message on Telegram. The first reward is guaranteed within 24 hours of your first successful visit.
2. Daily 1-Minute Commitment
Unlike other platforms that require hours of your time, CheckIn30 asks for just 60 seconds per day. Once you log in, the timer starts automatically - no buttons to press. You can browse other parts of the website or keep the tab open in the background. The progress bar shows you exactly how much time is left. After 60 seconds, a popup appears asking you to claim your streak for the day. It's that simple!
3. Unlimited Streak System
Your streak never resets - it keeps growing forever! Even after you cross 30 days, 60 days, 100 days, or even 365 days, your streak continues to increase. The visual streak dots show your progress up to 30 days, and beyond that we show arrows to indicate continuation. The longer your streak, the more valuable your rewards become. Many of our users have crossed 100+ days and are earning premium rewards.
4. 5-Day Milestone Rewards
Every 5 days is a special milestone - days 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, and so on. On these days, you earn a reward that is sent directly to your registered UPI ID within 24 hours. The reward amount increases as your streak grows. For example, your first milestone at day 5 gives a starter reward, but by day 30 you're in the premium tier. Each milestone is celebrated with a special popup and a Telegram notification with all your details.
5. Real-Time Telegram Updates
We use two dedicated Telegram bots to keep you informed. When you register, your details (name, email, UPI, time) are sent to our registration bot for record-keeping. When you hit a 5-day milestone, your complete details along with the streak information are sent to our rewards bot. This ensures transparency - you can verify that your milestone has been recorded. You can also join our public Telegram channel @chackin30 for announcements and community updates.
6. Secure & Private Local Storage
All your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This means no servers, no databases, and no risk of data breaches. Your information never leaves your phone except when we send milestone notifications to Telegram (with your consent). Even if you close the browser and come back tomorrow, you'll be automatically logged in if you selected "Keep me logged in". Different users on different phones have completely separate data.
